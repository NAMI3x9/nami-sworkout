Workout Instructions

  • Perform each exercise for 4 sets of 25 reps (or 25 seconds for holds/stretches).

  • Click on a set circle to mark it as completed.
  • Use the timer for timed exercises (Hollow holds, Plank, Crossover stretch).
  • Complete all sets of an exercise before moving to the next one.
  • Stay hydrated and maintain proper form throughout the workout.
